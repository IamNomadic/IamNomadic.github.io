WellBound

WellBound is a game with a randomly generated level every time you play it. The player wakes up in a strange grassy world surrounded by wilderness on all sides. The player must learn to survive to escape or solve the final puzzle.

Gameplay & Features

  • World Generation: Three biomes: desert, swamp, forest, generated with Perlin noise.
  • Difficulty System: Enemies gain speed, damage, and range over time.
  • Crafting System: Combine items to create stamina food, effects, or traps.
  • Ritual System: Complete sacrifice, resource, and sacred item rituals to escape.
